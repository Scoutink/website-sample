About Zhaoke Ophthalmology

Founded in 2017 and listed on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange (SEHK: 6622), Zhaoke Ophthalmology is a fully integrated ophthalmic platform spanning research & development, manufacturing, and commercialization of therapies for both front- and back-of-the-eye diseases.

With a state-of-the-art 7,600 m¬≤ GMP facility in Nansha New District, Guangzhou, Zhaoke focuses on six major eye diseases: dry eye disease (DED), myopia, presbyopia, wet age-related macular degeneration (wAMD), diabetic macular edema (DME), and glaucoma.
